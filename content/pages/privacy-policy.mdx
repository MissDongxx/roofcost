## Introduction

At RoofCostAI, accessible from [roofcostai.com](https://roofcostai.com), one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by RoofCostAI and how we use it.

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at **info@roofcostai.com**.

## Data Collection and Use

### Localization Data (ZIP Code)
To provide accurate roofing cost estimates tailored to your specific location, we collect your **ZIP Code**. This information is used solely for localization calculations, such as adjusting for regional labor and material costs. We **do not store** any Personal Identifiable Information (PII) in connection with your search queries.

### Cookies and Web Beacons
Like any other website, RoofCostAI uses "cookies". These cookies are used to store information including visitors' preferences, and the pages on the website that the visitor accessed or visited. The information is used to optimize the users' experience by customizing our web page content based on visitors' browser type and/or other information.

### Google Analytics
We use **Google Analytics** to understand how our website is used and to improve the user experience. Google Analytics collect information anonymously. It reports website trends without identifying individual visitors.

## Third-Party Services

### Google AdSense
We may use **Google AdSense** to display advertisements on our website. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our site or other sites on the Internet.

### HomeAdvisor Affiliate Program
RoofCostAI participates in the **HomeAdvisor Affiliate Program**. This means we may include affiliate links to HomeAdvisor. If you click on these links and connect with a contractor, we may earn a referral fee. This helps support our site and keeps our estimation tool free for users.

## Contact Information

If you would like to contact us for any reason regarding our privacy practices, please email us at **info@roofcostai.com**.
