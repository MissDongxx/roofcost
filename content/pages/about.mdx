## Our Mission

RoofCostAI was built to solve a common problem for homeowners: the lack of transparency when it comes to roofing costs. We believe that you should be able to get a professional-grade estimate of your roofing project without the pressure of a sales call.

Our tool is designed to be **transparent, unbiased, and completely free to use**.

## How It Works

We use advanced math and data modeling to calculate estimates based on your specific roof metrics and location. Our data sources include:

- **Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS)**: For current local labor rate data across the United States.
- **RSMeans material index**: For the latest pricing on roofing materials, from asphalt shingles to premium metal roofing.

By combining these data points with your roof's unique characteristics, we can provide a detailed cost breakdown that matches what a professional contractor might quote.
